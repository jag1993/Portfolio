About Me

Hello! I'm Jose Garcia, aspiring web developer and current student at the The Coding Bootcamp in UCLA. I recently moved to the United States from the Philippines in February of this year with my family in Nevada. I then decided to move out to LA to pursue further studies. Prior to me leaving I’ve always felt the need for a career change.

Out of curiosity, I tried learning web development as a hobby doing many tutorials in CSS, HTML, and some light Javascript. Eventually, I decided to try and pursue web development as a career. To do that, I felt like I needed more formal education and after thorough research I came across the coding bootcamp hosted in UCLA.
